ABOUT

We provide a collection of well detailed recipes for a variety of local cuisine. Our aim is to revolutionise the hospitality industry by establish an avenue that is easily accessible, through which our clients can view the easy and simple to follow recipes. Our menu is full reflective of our indentity by incooperating both modern and traditional cuisine. The recipes use ingredients that are readily available at the nearest local market or shop. Above all, we hope to deliver a service that reminds you of the good times, as most Kenyan dishes are be best enjoyed sharing style, the way we eat at home, with our closest family and friends.
